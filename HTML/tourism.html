Kenya's Tourism.

Kenya is widely known for its rich wildlife, beautiful scenaries and speedy runners.

Some of the major tourist attractions include:

  • The Maasai Mara
  • The Nairobi National Park
  • Coastal Beaches

Fast Runners.

Kenya is known for its fast runners who shine in both short and long distance races. We take pride in our long stride and extra ordinary stamina

One of the country's world class runners include Eliud Kipchoge who has shown his resilience in world marathons time and time again and has broken record upon records. Including some that he set himself. Others include David Rudisha and Mary Keitany

Beautiful Scenaries.

Kenya is a beautiful country blessed with natural resources and scenaries that can take your breath away

Famous regions in Kenya such as Mombasa serve as major tourist attractions showcasing the county's beautiful beaches and world class restaurants that can be located along the Mombasa coastal region.

In addition, the highland areas such as Mount Kenya has attracted hikers who dare challenge the height of the mountain.

Diverse Wildlife.

Kenya takes pride in its wildlife and the diverse animals and plants that it contains. Wildlife has been a major contribution to the increased amount of tourism that the country receives. I have personally takes trips to the Nairobi National Park and experienced our nature first-hand.

The majority of the wildlife can be found and viewed roaming freely in their natural habitat in areas such as:

  1. The Maasai Mara
  2. The Nairobi National Park
